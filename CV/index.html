Project 1: Livit

Sharkdreams is a US based fast growing startup which is providing digital solutions in terms of software and hardware to solve healthcare problems. Facilitating closer patient-provider communication. Enabling precision care, focused on the needs of the individual, while addressing universal challenges. Livit is the product of Sharkdreams which is a new approach to virtual care. It’s a digital clinic that lets patients and healthcare providers see medication use and health data instantly, continuously, and in real time. So they can make better, more informed decisions together. This Livit system has 2 major components Hardware and Software. Hardware: The Livit Hardware is universal, disposable, and lasts the duration of the prescription. It accurately detects medicine consumption, and securely transfers usage data to the system for private access by the patient and provider. Software: The Livit software system is comprised of Mobile App (patient facing) and Web App (Care provider facing) which will connect patients and healthcare providers. Together, they can review the patient’s health, conduct live chats, and even update prescriptions. All without an office visit. For patients who take multiple medications, the LIVIT app can send reminders and alerts to any changes in prescription or dosage.
